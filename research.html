Research

Liscere examines whether a protocol-valid and authorised industrial action is appropriate in its operational context. Protocol validity and access legitimacy are not sufficient to make that determination. The work below sets out the framework, an initial Modbus/TCP evaluation, and the laboratory in which it is carried out.

01

Framework

A protocol-agnostic chain for evaluating an industrial action across four dimensions: protocol operation, automation artefact, operational context, and policy constraint.

Evaluation
